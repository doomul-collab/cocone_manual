Accommodation Tax

Please place the accommodation tax in the envelope provided in your room before you leave.

  • Accommodation tax: 200 JPY per adult / per night
  • Children (12 and under): No charge

Example:
2 adults × 3 nights × 200 JPY = 1,200 JPY
