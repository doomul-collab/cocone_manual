House Rules

Quiet Hours

Please be especially quiet after 9:00 PM.
Keep TV, phone calls, and music volume low.

Smoking

No smoking indoors.
Smoking is only allowed in designated areas.

Visitors

Entry is limited to registered guests only.
Please refrain from inviting non-guests.

Garbage Separation

  • Burnable: paper, plastic bottles, plastic products
  • Non-burnable: cans, bottles, metal items

After separating, please place garbage together on the balcony.

Towels

Please use towels from the towel storage area.
After use, place used towels together.

Wi-Fi

Wi-Fi information is provided inside the room.

Laundry

The coin laundry is located past the entrance on the 1st floor,
in front of the bicycle storage area.
Detergent is available under the sink in your room.

Facilities & Equipment

If any furniture or equipment is damaged, please contact the host immediately.
Do not move or remove items without permission.

Safety (Earthquake / Fire)

In case of an earthquake or fire, please refer to the Safety Information page.

Check-out Courtesy

Please organize used dishes lightly and separate garbage before check-out.
