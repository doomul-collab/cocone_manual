House Rules During Stay

Our shared promise for a restful stay.
Thank you for your cooperation.

Noise

After 9:00 PM, please be especially mindful of noise.
Lower the volume of TV, calls, and music to avoid disturbing others.

Smoking

Smoking is strictly prohibited indoors.
Smoking is only allowed in designated smoking areas.

Visitors

Entry of non-registered guests is not permitted.
Please refrain from inviting visitors without prior approval.

Trash Separation

Please separate your trash properly.
Sort into Burnable and Non-burnable categories,
and leave them on the veranda.

  • Burnable Trash: Paper, PET bottles, plastic items
  • Non-burnable Trash: Cans, bottles, metal items

Laundry

The coin laundry is located past the 1st floor entrance,
in front of the bicycle parking area.

Detergent is stored under the room sink.
Feel free to use it.

Facility Usage

If any furniture or equipment is damaged, please contact the host immediately.
Unauthorized relocation or removal of items is prohibited.

Emergency Situations (Fire / Earthquake)

In the event of fire or earthquake, please refer to the safety guide page.

Go to Safety Guide (Fire/Earthquake)

Towel Use

Please use towels available in the towel storage.
Place used towels together in one spot.

Wi‑Fi

Please refer to the in-room guide for Wi‑Fi information.
