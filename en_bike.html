Bicycle Rental Information

As a guest of Cocone House, you may use our complimentary bicycles during your stay.
Before using a bicycle, please read the guidelines below and agree to the terms.

Where to Find the Bicycles

The bicycles are located in the bicycle storage area past the ground floor entrance.
Please use the bicycles marked with “cocone” on the rear wheel.

Keys

The keys are stored in a lockbox on the 3rd floor balcony near the emergency exit.
The lockbox code will be provided by the host after you agree to the terms.

Before You Ride

  • Check that the brakes are working properly.
  • Check tire pressure.
  • Make sure the seat is securely fixed.

If the tires need air, please use the pump located next to the lockbox.

Important Rules

1. Responsibility

Cocone House is not liable for any accidents, injuries, or damages to yourself or others during use.
By using the bicycle, you accept full responsibility for your own safety.

We recommend having travel insurance or liability insurance before using the bicycle.

2. In Case of Accident or Damage

If an accident or malfunction occurs, stop using the bicycle immediately.
You must contact both the police and the host if an accident occurs.

3. Keys & Bicycle Care

Be careful not to lose the key or bicycle.
Lost keys incur a replacement fee of 2,000 JPY.

4. Restrictions

  • No riding under the influence of alcohol.
  • Follow all traffic laws.
  • Be cautious when riding at night.
  • Do not lend or transfer the bicycle to others.

5. Agreement

Using a bicycle indicates that you fully understand and agree to the terms above.
