Bicycle Rental Information

Cocone House offers free bicycle rental for staying guests.
Please read the information below and proceed only if you agree to all terms.

Location of Bicycles

Bicycles are stored on the 1st floor inside the entrance area.
Please use the bicycles marked "cocone" on the rear wheel.

Key Storage

Keys are stored in a lockbox on the 3rd floor, near the emergency exit balcony.
The code will be provided after confirming your agreement.

Before You Ride — Check

  • Brake function
  • Tire pressure
  • Saddle height & security

If air is low, you may use the pump beside the lockbox.

Important Rules

1. Responsibility

We are not responsible for accidents, injuries, damages, or third-party issues
that may occur during use.
Starting to use the bicycle means you agree to these terms.

2. If an Accident or Issue Occurs

Please stop riding immediately and contact the host.
If an accident occurs, you must contact both the police and the host.

3. Keys & Management

Please take care not to lose the key.
If lost, a fee of 2,000 JPY will be charged.

4. Prohibited / Caution

  • No use after drinking alcohol
  • Follow traffic laws
  • Ensure lighting when riding at night
  • No lending or transferring to others

5. Meaning of Agreement

By beginning use, you confirm that you understand the rules and
accept full responsibility for your ride.
