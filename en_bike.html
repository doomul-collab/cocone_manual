Bicycle Use Information

Cocone House provides free bicycles for our guests.
Please read the information and rules below carefully before use.

Bicycle Location

Bicycles are located in the bicycle storage area past the entrance on the 1st floor.
Please use bicycles marked “cocone” on the rear wheel.

Bicycle Key

Bicycle keys are kept in a key box on the 3rd floor balcony near the emergency exit.
The PIN code for the key box will be provided by the host after you agree to the rules.

Check Before Use

  • Make sure the brakes are working properly.
  • Check that the tires have enough air.
  • Confirm that the saddle is firmly fixed.

If the tires need air, please use the air pump next to the key box.

Bicycle Use Rules (Important)

1. Responsibility

The host takes no responsibility for accidents, injuries, or damage to third parties during bicycle use.
Bicycles are used entirely at the guest’s own risk.

We strongly recommend having travel insurance and personal liability insurance.

2. Accidents or Damage

If an accident or damage occurs, please stop using the bicycle immediately and contact the host.
In case of an accident, please contact both the police and the host.

3. Key & Bicycle Management

Please take good care of the bicycle and key.
If the key is lost, a 2,000 JPY replacement fee will be charged.

4. Usage Rules

  • No riding under the influence of alcohol.
  • Please obey all traffic rules.
  • Be especially careful when riding at night.
  • Lending the bicycle to others is not allowed.

5. Agreement

By using the bicycle, you confirm that you understand and agree to all rules above
and accept full responsibility for bicycle use.
