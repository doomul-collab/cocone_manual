Bicycle Rental Information

Cocone House offers free bicycle rental for staying guests.
Please read the information below and proceed only if you agree to all conditions.

Storage Location

Bicycles are kept in the indoor storage space near the entrance on the 1st floor.
Look for the ones with “cocone” marked on the rear wheel.

Key Storage

Keys are stored in a lockbox located on the 3rd floor emergency exit balcony.
The code will be provided after confirming your agreement.

Before Use — Check

  • Brake function
  • Tire air pressure
  • Saddle height & stability

If air is low, please use the pump beside the lockbox.

Rules & Agreement (Important)

1. Responsibility

We cannot be held responsible for any accidents, injuries, damages, or third-party issues
that occur during use.
By starting to use the bicycle, you are considered to have agreed to these terms.

Travel/accident insurance is recommended if available.

2. In Case of Accident or Malfunction

Stop riding immediately and contact the host.
If an accident occurs, you must inform both the police and the host.

3. Keys & Management

Please be careful not to lose the key.
Lost keys: 2,000 JPY fee will be charged.

4. Prohibited / Caution

  • No use after drinking alcohol
  • Follow traffic laws & local regulations
  • Ensure proper lighting when riding at night
  • No lending or transferring to others

5. Meaning of Agreement

Starting usage means you understand all rules and
accept to use the bicycle at your own risk.
