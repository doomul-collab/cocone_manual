Safety Guide
(Fire · Earthquake)

In case of an emergency, please stay calm and follow the instructions below.

In Case of Fire

  • If you see smoke or fire, evacuate the building immediately.
  • Do not use the elevator — please use the stairs.
  • Once outside, contact the host from a safe location.

In Case of Earthquake

  • Protect yourself under a desk or a sturdy table until the shaking stops.
  • When the shaking stops, secure an exit and check your surroundings first.
  • Please avoid rushing outside; follow guidance and posted instructions.
