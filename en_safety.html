Safety Information (Earthquake / Fire)

In Case of Earthquake

  • Stay calm and protect yourself.
  • Take cover under a table or protect your head.
  • After the shaking stops, evacuate calmly using the emergency exit.

In Case of Fire

  • If you discover a fire, alert others immediately.
  • Keep low to avoid smoke while evacuating.
  • Do not use elevators. Use the emergency stairs.

Evacuation Route & Emergency Items
