Room Number

Your room number will be sent to you by the host before check-in.

Room Door PIN Code (Important)

The room door is secured with a digital door lock.

  • Guests who booked via Booking.com or Airbnb:
    Please enter the last 4 digits of the PIN code you registered at booking.
  • Guests who booked via other websites:
    The host will inform you of the PIN code separately.

IMPORTANT:
After entering the 4-digit PIN code,
you must press the * (asterisk) button.
The door will NOT open unless you press *.
