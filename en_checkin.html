Check-in Guide

Check-in is available from 15:00.

Self Check-in Guide

cocone house operates on a self check-in basis.
The host will send your room number via a private message before your arrival.

How to Enter

The entrance uses a digital door lock.

  • For Booking.com / Airbnb guests
    Enter the last 4 digits of the phone number used for the reservation + asterisk (*).
    Ex) 1234* → You must press the * after the numbers.
  • For other sites / Direct bookings
    The host will provide a separate password.
    After entering the password, press the * (asterisk) to unlock.
    ※ The door will not open unless you press both the numbers and the *.

※ If you have any issues, please contact the host at any time.
Providing your estimated arrival time in advance helps ensure a smooth check-in.
