Check-in Guide

Check-in is available from 15:00.

Self Check-in Information

Cocone House operates on a self check-in basis.
Your room number will be provided by the host via an individual message before your arrival.

How to Enter

The entrance is equipped with a digital door lock.

  • If you booked via Booking.com / Airbnb
    You can open the door by entering the last 4 digits of the phone number you registered during booking, followed by the asterisk (*).
    e.g., 1234* → You must press * after the numbers.
  • Other sites / Direct Booking
    The host will provide you with a separate password.
    After entering the password, make sure to press * (asterisk) to open the door.
    ※ The door will open only after pressing both the numbers and the asterisk (*).

※ If you have any problems, please feel free to contact the host.
Informing us of your estimated arrival time in advance will help ensure a smooth check-in.
