Getting Here

View Cocone House on Google Maps

By Subway

Take the Fukuoka Subway Airport Line and get off at Tojinmachi Station (K05).
Exit via Exit 6 and walk straight for about 30 meters.
Turn right at the Fukuoka Bank and walk about 10 meters.
Enter the alley between the dental clinic and the blue vending machine — the entrance is ahead.
