Access & Directions

By Subway

Take the Fukuoka City Subway Airport Line and get off at
Tojinmachi Station (Station No. K05).
Exit from Exit 6 and walk straight for about 30 meters.
Turn right at the Fukuoka Bank corner and walk about 10 meters.
The entrance is between a dental clinic and a blue vending machine.
