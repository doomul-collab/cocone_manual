Check-in Guide

Check-in is available from 15:00.

Self Check-in Information

cocone house operates on a self check-in basis.
The host will send your room number via a private message before check-in.

How to Enter

The entrance is equipped with a digital door lock.

  • For reservations via Booking.com / Airbnb
    You can open the door by entering the last 4 digits of the phone number you registered, followed by the star key (*).
    e.g., 1234* → You must press the * after the numbers.
  • For other sites / Direct bookings
    The host will provide you with a separate PIN code.
    After entering the PIN, be sure to press the * (star key) to open the door.
    ※ The door will not open unless you press both the numbers and the * key.

※ If you encounter any problems, please feel free to contact the host.
Informing us of your estimated arrival time in advance helps with a smooth check-in.
